
Hatha Yoga: Focuses on physical postures and breathing techniques, making it a good introduction for beginners.








Vinyasa Yoga: Involves a dynamic flow of poses synchronized with breath, emphasizing movement and transition.

Ashtanga Yoga: A structured, fast-paced style that follows a specific sequence of postures and is known for its rigorous practice.

Iyengar Yoga: Emphasizes alignment and precision in poses, often using props like blocks and straps for support.

Bikram Yoga: Consists of a set series of poses practiced in a heated room, aimed at detoxification and flexibility.

Kundalini Yoga: Focuses on awakening spiritual energy through breath, chanting, and specific movements, integrating mind, body, and spirit.
